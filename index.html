ABOUT CREDO

A Partnership to Improve Trauma Outcomes for Patients, Families, Responders, and Communities

ABOUT

The CREDO consortium brings together an incredible group of leaders and experts, clinicians,researchers, educators, policymakers and businessmen from across European and international hospitals, universities, non-governmental organizations, and industry. We work to enhance quality of care and patient safety in disasters, emergencies, and traumatic (DT) events by developing a harmonised framework for D/T care and risk management that (1) allows stakeholders to more effectively assess dynamic multi-hazard risks, (2) delivers new training in leadership and teambased care to best protect the physical and emotional wellness of healthcare workers across multiple hazards, sectors, scales, and goals, and (3) provides adaptive learning, precision diagnostics, human factor shaping resilience, and patient and user-based design approaches

OUR VISION

CREDO will expedite industry-wide coordination, collaboration, and cooperation on disasters and trauma safety and training by organizing a series of seminars and satellite symposia during 2022 to 2023 to discuss the global pandemic response, lessons for our recovery, and how we can build resilience to current and future health threats. The workshops will highlight the potential benefits of new digital solutions to the key priority areas of disaster and trauma prevention and management, such as the safety of patients, healthcare providers, vulnerable users, consumer products and services

THEMATIC AREAS

CREDO offers innovative, cross-cutting collaborations, working closely with leading clinical, simulation, and education providers to advance the safety, quality, and value of healthcare. We exist to serve a greater purpose: delivering applied research programs and knowledge transfer to engender an ethos of inquisitiveness and innovation, building upon the rich cultural, medical, and scientific heritage of the European Union.
